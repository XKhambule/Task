The history of second wave mobile

The second wave mobile was founded by a 21 year old student Xolani on the 15th of July 2023.
Second Wave Mobile was founded on 15 July 2023 with a simple but powerful idea which was to make quality mobile phones affordable for everyone.
The business began when the founder purchased a new phone while already owning two devices that were still in excellent working condition.
Rather than letting them go to waste, he decided to sell them at reasonable prices to people who needed reliable phones but could not afford brand-new ones.

What started as a small personal decision quickly turned into something much bigger.
The first two sales were successful, and the customers were extremely satisfied with the quality and affordability of the phones.
They appreciated not only the lower prices, but also the honesty and transparency about the condition of each device.
Through word of mouth, more people began to show interest, and it became clear that there was a growing demand for trustworthy pre-owned phones.

Motivated by this response, the founder began researching the market and learning basic repair skills.
He started sourcing pre-owned phones, including slightly damaged ones, and restoring them to good working condition.
Each phone is carefully tested, cleaned, and repaired where necessary to ensure it meets a high standard before being sold.
This commitment to quality helps build trust with customers and sets the business apart from competitors.

Second Wave Mobile is not just about selling phones, it is about giving devices a second life and helping people stay connected in an affordable way.
The business also promotes environmental sustainability by reducing electronic waste and encouraging the reuse of technology.

Today, Second Wave Mobile continues to grow, aiming to expand its product range and reach more customers through both physical sales and an online platform.
The long-term vision is to become a trusted brand known for affordability, reliability, and excellent customer service, while making a positive impact in the community.

Through dedication, honesty, and a clear purpose, Second Wave Mobile has transformed from a simple idea into a promising and meaningful business venture.

The mission of second wave mobile

Our mission is to provide affordable, reliable, and good-quality pre-owned mobile phones by carefully sourcing devices, repairing damaged units, and making sure each phone meets clear quality standards before resale.
We are committed to giving every device a second life through proper refurbishment, careful testing, and honest descriptions so that customers can buy with confidence.

We aim to make technology available to more people, especially those who cannot afford brand-new smartphones, while still offering good performance and value for money.
By providing fair prices, exclusive deals, and customer-friendly promotions, we aim to build strong, long-term relationships with our customers.

In addition, Second Wave Mobile is committed to supporting environmental sustainability by reducing electronic waste and encouraging the reuse of mobile devices.
We believe in running our business with honesty, openness, and a strong focus on customer satisfaction, making sure every customer receives a product they can trust and depend on.

The vision of second wave

Our vision is to build a successful and growing business with a trusted five-star reputation, known for reliability, honesty, and excellent customer service.
We aim to become a popular and preferred choice for pre-owned mobile phones by consistently meeting customer needs and going beyond their expectations.

We want to grow our business both locally and online, reaching more customers and increasing sales through good-quality products, fair pricing, and excellent service.
Our goal is to create a brand that customers recognise and trust, where they feel confident in every purchase they make.

In the long term, we aim to lead in the pre-owned phone market by setting high standards for quality, affordability, and customer care, while also making a positive contribution to the community and encouraging sustainable business practices.
